Channa Dal Tikkis

  • 2
  • 8
  • 10min
  • 10min

Instant healthy tikkis that are quick to make and full of nutrients and proteins

Ingredients

Main Ingredients

Cooking Steps

Steps

Step 1 :

Coarsely grind chana dal.

Step 2 :

In bowl, add grinded channa dal and add mashed boiled potato.

Step 3 :

Mix it well and then add onion, salt, black pepper, green chillies, and red chilli powder.

Step 4 :

Take dal mixture in hand and make small tikki.

Step 5 :

On non-stick tawa, shallow fry Channa Dal Tikkis and serve hot with mint chutney and tomato ketchup.
