New on Allrecipes: Order Your Recipe Ingredients Online With One Click

Shop your favorite recipes with grocery delivery or pickup at your local Walmart.

By Allrecipes Editorial Team
Published on April 9, 2021
Trending Videos

No matter how well you organize your shopping list, perusing grocery store shelves for recipe ingredients can take as much time as cooking, or even longer. Knowing you have enough on your plate, Allrecipes has partnered with Walmart to make grocery shopping quicker and easier than ever. With the click of a button, you can now add ingredients from thousands of your favorite Allrecipes dishes directly to your Walmart shopping cart for delivery or pickup. Here's how it works:

Click to buy

Look for the 'Buy Ingredients' button on select recipes and add ingredients directly to your online shopping cart with one click.

Select your local store

Choose curbside pickup or delivery from one of 3,000+ Walmart stores in the United States.

See prices and availability in real time

You'll know exactly which brands, products, and sizes are available at your local store and how much you're paying.

Buy exactly what you want

If you don't need one of the ingredients listed, you can easily remove it from the cart with a click. If you prefer another brand, click "switch" by the ingredient to choose one that aligns with your tastes or budget. Then click "checkout" and go to your Walmart cart.
